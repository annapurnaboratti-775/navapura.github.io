About Navapura

A sustainable smart-city concept developed for the DAA project.

Vision

Navapura envisions a future-ready city that blends sustainability, efficient mobility, and vibrant mixed-use neighborhoods. Its planning focuses on minimizing carbon footprint while improving quality of life.

Objectives

  • Sustainable water and energy systems
  • Transit-oriented development
  • Green corridors and open spaces
  • Balanced zoning to minimize commute distances
  • Inclusive and accessible urban design

Methodology

The city plan was refined through zoning analysis, transport network modelling, node hierarchy design, and environmental integration.

Project Info

City Area: ~45 sq. km
Target Population: ~350,000
Design Horizon: 2040
Planning Layers: Zoning, Transit, Green Infra, Utilities
