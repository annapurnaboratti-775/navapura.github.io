City Design Overview

Zoning · Mobility · Sustainability · Utilities · Public Space

Zoning Strategy

Navapura uses a multi-core zoning model with mixed-use nodes, industrial corridors separated by green buffers, and high-density TOD rings around transit stations.

  • Mixed-use: 35%
  • Residential: 30%
  • Commercial: 20%
  • Industrial: 10%
  • Institutional/Civic: 5%

Transport Hierarchy

The movement network prioritizes sustainable modes:

  • MRT corridors linking major activity centers
  • Dedicated BRT and city buses
  • Continuous cycle-track grid
  • Pedestrianized commercial blocks

Green Infrastructure Design

Green corridors connect parks, floodplains, and ecological buffers. Stormwater systems include bioswales and permeable pavements.

Utilities & Services

Water, power, and waste systems are integrated into a smart grid with redundancy and real-time monitoring.
